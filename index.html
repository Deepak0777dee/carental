Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about renting a car with us.

What do I need to rent a car?

You need a valid driver's license, a credit card in the primary driver's name, and proof of insurance. International renters may also need a passport and an International Driving Permit.

Is there an age requirement?

Yes, the minimum age to rent is typically 21 years old. Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge and may be restricted from renting certain premium vehicle classes.

Can I return the car to a different location?

Absolutely! We offer one-way rentals. Simply select your preferred return location during the booking process. Please note that a one-way drop-off fee may apply depending on the locations.
